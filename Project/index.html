WHO WE ARE

We Started in 2009, basically as soft drinks shop, which later became the now famous Shakes Parlour in Goa. In 2009, we started serving milkshakes snacks and juice items.

The shop has an array of milkshakes. There is the regular fair of vanilla,mango strawberry and chocolate milkshakes available and there are others juices available made from fresh fruits. The hottest selling chilled milkshakes here are banana shakes, cold coffee and watermelon juice.
